What is Mario Party?

Mario Party is a couch party game where you and friends can play each other for fun. You attempt to collect stars by beating challenges or gathering enough coins to purchase them.

Why Should you play it?

Mario Party helps to showcase a great vareity of game mechanics in very short mini games. It also helps illustrate the extra fun that can be added by being able to play with friends that you can see and talk to easily. Additonally it serves as another example of a game that has changed little in its core mechanic over the years and continued to do very well.
